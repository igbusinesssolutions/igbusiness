    Who are we?

    Mukuni Ward is found in Katombora Constituency, Southern Province. The Health section was founded with initiative of Mukuni Chiefdom leadership in collaboration with Mukuni Health Clinic in 2010 with focus on health related issues. Our team include Health personnels, Teachers, Agriculture Officer, prominent Businessmen, etc. and currently membership is 21.
    Our core business is ensure that 'residents live a health life'; are sensitized with latest information related to health. Mukuni Health Clinic and the Butterfly Tree Zambia play a key roles in this ward.

    Covid is real.

    Covid is real and has affected the whole world economically, socially and spiritually and Zambia is not unexceptional. Let's unite and fight covid because its real. Follow the covid guidelines as published by Ministry of Health.
    1. Wash your hands
    2. Maintain physical distance
    4. Wash your hands frequently or use hand sanitizer
    5. Avoid crowded places, stay at home
    3. Seek medical attention if symptomatic
    Above all, be tested and get jabbed.

    Keep Mukuni Community Clean

    Keeping your streets clean also creates a positive impression of your communities and neighborhoods and can inspire people to do their part to keep the streets clean for everyone. Use available garbage cans regularly as Council empties them every week. You can also share information on the risks of dumping waste and garbage in the streets and educate people in your community on how to properly dispose of all types of home, garden and yard waste. Your community deserves safe and healthy streets free from garbage, dirt and debris.
